What is Shelf.com?

For apparel you want to purchase, Shelf.com will: (a) manage all your items in one location (a unified view of your wishlist), (b) save money (find, and apply promotions), (c) find similar items at other stores and their prices, and (d) track prices and make when-to-buy recommendations.
